Employee Dataset Documentation

This dataset contains information about company employees across different departments.

Purpose:
This dataset is used for HR analytics and workforce management. It tracks employee demographics,
compensation, and tenure information.

Column Descriptions:
- id: A unique identifier assigned to each employee in the system
- name: Full name of the employee (first name and last name)
- age: Current age of the employee in years
- department: The business unit or department where the employee works (Engineering, Marketing, or Sales)
- salary: Annual salary in USD before taxes and deductions
- hire_date: The date when the employee was hired in YYYY-MM-DD format

Data Collection:
This data is extracted from the company's HR management system on a monthly basis.
The dataset is maintained by the Human Resources department and updated regularly.

Usage Notes:
- All employee information should be handled according to company privacy policies
- Salary information is confidential and should only be used for authorized analytics
- The dataset represents a snapshot at a specific point in time
