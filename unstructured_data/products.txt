Product Inventory Dataset

This dataset contains the current inventory of products available in our warehouse system.

Overview:
The product catalog includes various items across different categories including Electronics,
Furniture, and Stationery. This data is used for inventory management, procurement planning,
and sales analytics.

Field Information:
- product_id: Unique identifier code for each product (format: P + 3-digit number)
- product_name: Descriptive name of the product
- category: Product classification (Electronics, Furniture, or Stationery)
- price: Current retail price in USD with two decimal places
- stock_quantity: Number of units currently available in the warehouse
- supplier: Name of the company that supplies this product

Data Maintenance:
The inventory data is synchronized with the warehouse management system every 6 hours.
Stock quantities reflect real-time availability and are updated as sales occur and
shipments are received.

Business Context:
Products with stock quantity below 30 units trigger automatic reorder notifications.
Prices are reviewed quarterly and may be adjusted based on supplier costs and market conditions.
The supplier field is critical for procurement and supply chain management processes.
